About Us

At S&R, we believe in creating mouthwatering meals that are full of flavor and tradition. Our pork and beef siomai are made with the finest ingredients, ensuring that every bite is pure perfection.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the joy of authentic Filipino siomai and rice to everyone, everywhere. We strive to deliver exceptional service and quality dishes that everyone can enjoy!

Our History

Founded in 2025 by a family passionate about Filipino cuisine, S&R has quickly become a local favorite. Our commitment to freshness and quality is at the heart of every dish we make.
